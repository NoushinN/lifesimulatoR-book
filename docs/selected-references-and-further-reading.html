Chapter 9 Selected References and Further Reading

This section collects selected readings recommended throughout the book. These sources provide background on origin-of-life theories, molecular evolution, protocells, autocatalytic networks, complexity, and astrobiology.

9.1 Origin of Life and General Frameworks

  • Deamer, D. W. (2019). Assembling Life: How Can Life Begin on Earth and Other Habitable Planets?
  • Luisi, P. L. (2016). The Emergence of Life: From Chemical Origins to Synthetic Biology.
  • Maynard Smith, J., & Szathmáry, E. (1999). The Origins of Life: From the Birth of Life to the Origin of Language.
  • Smith, E., & Morowitz, H. J. (2016). The Origin and Nature of Life on Earth.

9.2 RNA World and Molecular Evolution

  • Eigen, M. (1971). Self-Organization of Matter and the Evolution of Biological Macromolecules.
  • Gilbert, W. (1986). The RNA World.
  • Joyce, G. F. (2002). The Antiquity of RNA-Based Evolution.
  • Orgel, L. E. (2004). Prebiotic Chemistry and the Origin of the RNA World.

9.3 Protocells and Synthetic Life

  • Deamer, D. W. (2019). Assembling Life.
  • Morowitz, H. J. (1992). Beginnings of Cellular Life.
  • Szostak, J. W., Bartel, D. P., & Luisi, P. L. (2001). Synthesizing Life.

9.4 Autocatalytic Networks and Complexity

  • Hordijk, W., & Steel, M. (2018). Autocatalytic Sets and the Origin of Life.
  • Kauffman, S. A. (1993). The Origins of Order.
  • Lancet, D., Zidovetzki, R., & Markovitch, O. (2018). Systems Protobiology: Origin of Life in Lipid Catalytic Networks.
  • Walker, S. I. (2017). Origins of Life and Complexity.

9.5 Prebiotic Chemistry and Early Earth Environments

  • Martin, W., Baross, J., Kelley, D., & Russell, M. J. (2008). Hydrothermal Vents and the Origin of Life.
  • Miller, S. L. (1953). A Production of Amino Acids Under Possible Primitive Earth Conditions.
  • Sutherland, J. D. (2016). The Origin of Life—Out of the Blue.
  • Wächtershäuser, G. (1988). Before Enzymes and Templates: Theory of Surface Metabolism.
